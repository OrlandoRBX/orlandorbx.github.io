What is Orlando?

Orlando is a roleplay city game that is currently in development. We are planning to launch around 2K18 with a nice, polished, scripted and well-built game. Orlando all started with inspiration from communities and games like: Jailbreak and Hidden Developers. During development we are thinking of as many plans as we can to get ways to make the game more realistic.
